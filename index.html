  • What's counted: Direct contributions (Schedule B disbursements) made by pro-Israel PACs to federal candidate committees, plus independent expenditures (Schedule E) from pro-Israel Super PACs supporting or opposing federal candidates, as reported in FEC filings for the 2026 election cycle.

  • What's excluded: Lobbying, dark money, and non-federal races.
