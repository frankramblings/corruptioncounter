Methodology

  • What's counted: Direct contributions (Schedule B disbursements) made by pro-Israel PACs to federal candidate committees (House, Senate, and Presidential), as reported in FEC filings for the 2026 election cycle.
  • PACs included: AIPAC PAC (C00797670), NORPAC, JStreetPAC, DMFI PAC, JACPAC, NACPAC, Desert Caucus, To Protect Our Heritage PAC, Republican Jewish Coalition PAC, Pro-Israel America PAC, Florida Congressional Committee, Hudson Valley PAC, CityPAC, Citizens Organized PAC, Maryland Assoc. for Concerned Citizens, St Louisians for Better Government, National PAC, World Alliance for Israel, and Christians for Israel PAC — based on OpenSecrets' Pro-Israel industry list.
  • What's excluded: Independent expenditures (Super PAC spending), lobbying, dark money, and non-federal races.
  • Update frequency: Data is refreshed hourly from the FEC's OpenFEC API.
